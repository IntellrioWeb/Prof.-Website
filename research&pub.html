Research Interests

  • Planning and design of water-related services, Geospatial technologies like Remote Sensing, GIS & satellite-based positioning.
  • Land use/land cover change modelling & predictions, Integrated water resources planning & management.
  • Climate change impact on water resources and optimum land use planning.
  • Urban Water Management Modelling (supply and demand integration).
  • Remote sensing and GIS-based rainfall-runoff modelling.
  • Geospatial modelling for surveillance and monitoring of vector borne diseases.
